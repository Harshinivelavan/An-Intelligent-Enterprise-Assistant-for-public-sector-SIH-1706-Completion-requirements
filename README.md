Problem Title:
  An Intelligent Enterprise Assistant for public sector (SIH 1706)

Problem Description:

Large public sector organizations often face challenges in efficiently addressing the numerous queries raised by employees regarding HR policies, IT support, organizational events, and other workplace-related information. Employees usually depend on emails, manuals, or support teams to obtain answers, which can lead to delays, increased workload for support staff, and reduced productivity. Additionally, employees may need assistance in understanding important organizational documents, such as policy manuals or guidelines, which can be lengthy and difficult to quickly interpret.

To address these challenges, this project proposes the development of an intelligent chatbot using Natural Language Processing (NLP) and deep learning techniques. The chatbot will enable employees to interact with a conversational system that can understand their queries and provide accurate responses related to HR policies, IT support issues, company events, and other organizational matters. The system will also include document processing capabilities that allow employees to upload documents for summarization and keyword extraction, enabling faster access to relevant information.

Idea:

The main idea of this project is to develop an intelligent chatbot that helps employees in a large public sector organization quickly find answers to their questions. Employees often have queries related to HR policies, IT support, company events, and other organizational matters. Instead of contacting support staff or searching through lengthy documents, employees can interact with the chatbot through a simple conversational interface. The chatbot uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) and deep learning techniques to understand user questions and provide relevant responses from a predefined knowledge base.
